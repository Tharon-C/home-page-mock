"Transforming Science through Data-driven Discovery"

CyVerse is funded by the National Science Foundation’s Directorate for Biological Sciences. Our mission is to design, deploy, and expand a national cyberinfrastructure for life sciences research and train scientists in its use.

Our Products About CyVerse
Our Products

The ultimate goal is to provide the life sciences community with a robust cyberinfrastructure platform that enables scientific discovery through research and computational thinking.

Explore CyVerse
Developing solutions for today’s grand scientific challenges means we must understand how the organisms that contribute to our food, fuels, and ecosystem are shaped by interactions with their environment. CyVerse provides life scientists with powerful computational infrastructure to handle massive datasets and complex analyses, thus enabling data-driven discovery and collaborations. Our powerful extensible platforms provide data management, bioinformatics tools, image analyses, cloud services, APIs, and more.
View Product List View Brochure
Expand CyVerse
We are continually expanding our infrastructure capabilities and resources, partnering with third parties and comparable projects to transform data science in the digital age. Through our Powered by CyVerse program, third-party projects can leverage our cyberinfrastructure to provide services to their users, including a linked authentication system allowing secure single sign-on between their application and all CyVerse services, access to the Data Store, and the ability to execute analyses on high-performance computing resources.

Cereal Genomics Workshop

October 19, 2016 – October 25, 2016

This one-week workshop in Cereal Genomics will enable participants to take advantage of emerging genetic tools and the completed cereal genome sequences of most of the major cereal crops.
